CloudNova — Password Reset Guide
==================================

If you are unable to log in to your CloudNova account, follow these steps to reset your password.

STEP 1: REQUEST A PASSWORD RESET
---------------------------------
1. Go to https://console.cloudnova.io/login
2. Click "Forgot Password?" below the login form
3. Enter the email address associated with your CloudNova account
4. Click "Send Reset Link"
5. You will receive a password reset email within 5 minutes

STEP 2: CHECK YOUR EMAIL
--------------------------
1. Look for an email from "noreply@cloudnova.io" with subject "Password Reset Request"
2. If you don't see it, check your spam/junk folder
3. The reset link is valid for 1 hour
4. If the link has expired, request a new one by repeating Step 1

STEP 3: CREATE A NEW PASSWORD
-------------------------------
1. Click the reset link in the email
2. Enter your new password
3. Password requirements:
   - Minimum 12 characters
   - At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
   - At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
   - At least one number (0-9)
   - At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
   - Cannot reuse your last 5 passwords
4. Confirm the new password
5. Click "Reset Password"

STEP 4: VERIFY YOUR IDENTITY (if MFA is enabled)
---------------------------------------------------
1. After resetting your password, you may be asked to verify with MFA
2. Enter the code from your authenticator app
3. If you've lost access to your MFA device, use a backup recovery code
4. If you don't have backup codes, see "Account Recovery" below

ACCOUNT RECOVERY (No Access to Email or MFA)
-----------------------------------------------
If you cannot access your registered email OR your MFA device:

1. Go to https://support.cloudnova.io/account-recovery
2. Fill out the Account Recovery Form with:
   - Your full name
   - Organization name
   - Last known email address
   - Date of account creation (approximate)
   - Last 4 digits of the payment method on file
3. Upload a government-issued photo ID for identity verification
4. Our security team will review your request within 24-72 hours
5. You will be contacted via an alternate email address you provide

COMMON ISSUES
--------------
Q: I never received the reset email.
A: Check spam/junk folders. Ensure you're using the correct email. Try adding noreply@cloudnova.io to your contacts and request again.

Q: The reset link says "expired."
A: Reset links are valid for 1 hour. Request a new one from the login page.

Q: My new password is rejected.
A: Ensure it meets all requirements listed above. You cannot reuse your last 5 passwords.

Q: My account is locked.
A: After 5 failed login attempts, accounts are locked for 30 minutes. After 10 failed attempts, contact your organization admin to unlock the account.

Q: I'm locked out and I'm the only admin.
A: Contact CloudNova Support at support@cloudnova.io or call +1-800-CLOUDNOVA with your account ID and identity verification.

SECURITY TIPS
--------------
- Use a unique password not shared with other services
- Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for added security
- Store backup recovery codes in a secure password manager
- Rotate your password every 90 days
- Never share your password via email or chat
